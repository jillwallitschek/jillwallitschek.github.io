About:

The day to day operations of the Prairie Fields HOA is run by an all-volunteer Board of Directors along with help from many other committee volunteers. The Board maintains and improves HOA property and offer programs which are of value to Prairie Fields residents to increase the value of the neighborhood. Specific committes within the HOA help to maintain architecture, host exciting events, ensure easy communication, welcome new members, and elect new board members. Please browse our site for more information.

For information regaurding the Prairie Fields HOA Bylaws go here.

Events:

While we work hard to maintain the neighborhood and improve property value, we also host lots of fun events for our community. Some of these events include block parties, international potlucks, and June garage sales. For our seasonal events we have a 4th of July parade, a Savoy Orchard day in September, a Halloween parade, a Diwali Celebration, and Holiday Caroling. If you have an idea for an event, you can email events@prairiefields.org.
